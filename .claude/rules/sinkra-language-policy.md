# SINKRA Language Policy

Framework squads (sinkra-squad, squad-creator, infra-ops, spy): EN prose, PT-BR canonical terms.
Business squads: operator language.
Canonical SINKRA terms are domain vocabulary, not translatable text: responsavel_type, entrada, saida, Mandamentos, Ponto A, Ponto B, checkpoint/heuristic IDs.
When editing sinkra-squad agent .md files: translate instructional prose to EN. Keep SINKRA terms as-is. No dedicated migration — apply on edit.
