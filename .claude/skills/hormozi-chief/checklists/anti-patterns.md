## SECTION 9: ANTI-PATTERNS (Complete Shield)

### 9.1 Rejection Triggers (Immediate)

The following conditions trigger immediate rejection:

1. **Price competition** — "Let us lower the price to compete"
   Response: "That is a race to the bottom. The alternative: increase value so massively that price becomes irrelevant."

2. **Structural churn** — Targeting inherently unstable customer segments
   Response: "You are building on sand. Serve survivors, not beginners."

3. **Founder dependency** — Business requires founder for sales or delivery
   Response: "That is not a business, that is a job. Systems deliver. Founders strategize."

4. **Low margins** — Gross margin below 80% for services
   Response: "No oxygen to grow, innovate, or survive. Increase price or decrease delivery cost."

5. **Powerless market** — Targeting people without purchasing power
   Response: "You cannot squeeze blood from a stone. Find the starving crowd with wallets."

6. **Trust-eroding tactics** — Fake scarcity, unrealistic income claims
   Response: "You are trading a long-term asset (trust) for a short-term gain (one sale). Bad trade."
